For every countable things

Measure and share your moments

Countable is for record every moments that we meet in life. You can score key points and measure time shares in sports games or board games like chess. Even in debate class, Countable is useful to balance between debaters.

Graph is provided for interval and share, you can add CSV export feature for more detailed analysis.

Case 1. Pitching analysis

Mr. McKinze have a son, who hope to be a baseball player.

He willing take a holiday if his son pitch in the match. He try to never miss it.
Mr. McKinze launches Countable app right after enter ballpark to record son's pitching pattern.

When the game's over, Mr. McKinze have a discussion with his son about the kind of ball, pitching interval and pattern watching Countable app's record.

Archiving CSV files to Computer, Mr. McKinze believes these data makes son's dream come closer.

Case 2. Time balancing in debate

Highschool teacher Larry having debate classes recently.
Making a team and sharing opinions for a issue is good for learning on its own.

Larry opens Countable app in the beginning of the class.

With Counntable, Larry can balance opportunity of speaking on each side. And the count of exchange tells the heat of debate.

Larry also checks his time to be a good host.

After the class, Larry print out the time share graph and timeline to attach to class board.
Reviewing itself can be a good chance of learning.

Case 3. Real time information service of game

Jerry, working for a football club, planning to renew the website for a new season.

To meet the expectations of the fans, club set the bar higher as they give detailed data real time on website.

Jerry adopted custom solution of Countable and set more scorekeeper to count every situations of game.

Counted data published on the website of club in real time, attracted more visitors.
Nowadays even sports reporters and sports section manager in portal site request the real time data for their own use.
