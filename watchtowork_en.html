How many Pomodoros done this month?

To keep you focused on work

WatchToWork is an app for 'Pomodoro Technique' help you focused on your work. Pomodoro Technique (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pomodoro_Technique) Increase efficiency with cycle of 25min work - 5min break.

Features

  • - Record daily Pomodoro
  • - Set timer tick sound
  • - 1 minute left chime
  • - Display total Pomodoros and time
  • - Display monthly Pomodoros and time
